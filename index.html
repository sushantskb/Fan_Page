WICKET KEEPING

Dhoni holds the records of the most dismissals in an innings (6) and career (432) by an Indian wicket-keeper. Dhoni has the most stumpings (123) by any wicket-keeper in an ODI career, and is so far the only keeper to pass 100 stumpings.
